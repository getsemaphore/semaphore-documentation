### Company Identifiers

Italy's implementation provides comprehensive company identification through multiple registry codes:

- **VAT** (`partita IVA`): Primary business identifier for tax purposes
- **taxCode** (`codice fiscale`): Italian fiscal code, may differ from VAT for some entities
- **cciaa**: Chamber of Commerce code indicating the provincial chamber
- **reaCode**: Economic and Administrative Register number

### Search Capabilities

- **By company name** (contains matching): `search("Barilla")`
- **By VAT code** (with or without IT prefix): `search("01654010345")` or `search("IT01654010345")`
- **By tax code**: `search("03943150270")`

### Activity Classifications

Italy provides comprehensive activity classification through multiple nomenclatures:

**Primary Classifications (API-sourced):**

- **ATECO** (2007 & 2022): Italian standard industrial classification
- **SIC**: US Standard Industrial Classification codes
- **RAE**: Italian Economic Administrative Register classification
- **SAE**: Italian Statistical Administrative classification

**AI-Enhanced Classifications:**

- **NACE**: European standard classification (AI-inferred from ATECO codes)
- **ISIC**: International Standard Industrial Classification (AI-inferred)

The system filters out "ND99" (Not Available) codes and provides formal parsing for structured data with AI inference for international standards, ensuring comprehensive activity coverage while maintaining data quality.
